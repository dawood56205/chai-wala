Chai is one of the most popular and loved drinks, especially in countries like Pakistan and India. It is made by boiling tea leaves with milk, sugar, and sometimes spices like cardamom, cinnamon, or ginger. Chai has a rich taste and a pleasant aroma that makes it very refreshing. People enjoy chai at different times of the day, especially in the morning or evening. It is often served to guests as a sign of hospitality. In many homes, chai is not just a drink but a part of daily life and culture. Friends and family often gather together and share conversations over a cup of chai. Chai also provides energy and helps people feel relaxed. Whether at home, in a café, or at roadside stalls, chai brings comfort and warmth. It is more than just a beverage; it is a symbol of tradition, connection, and enjoyment. ☕
